## problem_0043 unsolved
The number, 1406357289, is a 0 to 9 pandigital number because it is made up of
each of the digits 0 to 9 in some order, but it also has a rather interesting
sub-string divisibility property.

Let _d_ 1 be the 1st digit, _d_ 2 be the 2nd digit, and so on. In this way, we
note the following:

  * _d_ 2 _d_ 3 _d_ 4=406 is divisible by 2
  * _d_ 3 _d_ 4 _d_ 5=063 is divisible by 3
  * _d_ 4 _d_ 5 _d_ 6=635 is divisible by 5
  * _d_ 5 _d_ 6 _d_ 7=357 is divisible by 7
  * _d_ 6 _d_ 7 _d_ 8=572 is divisible by 11
  * _d_ 7 _d_ 8 _d_ 9=728 is divisible by 13
  * _d_ 8 _d_ 9 _d_ 10=289 is divisible by 17

Find the sum of all 0 to 9 pandigital numbers with this property.
